# Types of Compilers

x86-64

 - (amd64, i64, etc)
 - extra 32 bits on Intel's x86 architecture
 - variable instruction length (1-16 bytes)
 - multi register access. eg lower bound of rax

---

 - RAX
    - EAX   (lower 32 bits)
    - AX    (lower 16 bits of EAX)
    - AL/AH (lower/higher 8 bits of AX)
